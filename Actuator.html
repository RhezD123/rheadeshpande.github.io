Actuator PCB

A custom actuator control PCB developed to drive both the Robotic Gripper Arm and Torpedo systems for the competition-grade AUV built by UBC's Marine Robotics Engineering Design Team.

Project Description

This board is engineered to withstand 17V and 20A to drive five servos and two linear actuators, featuring galvanic isolation to protect logic circuits from battery noise. It utilizes SPI for internal communication and CAN bus for stable connectivity across the AUV’s subsystems. The design prioritizes compact power delivery and noise reduction to ensure precise control.
