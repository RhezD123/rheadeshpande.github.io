Project Description

This actuator PCB was designed to drive and control electric actuators with high precision and reliability. The board integrates power regulation, motor driver circuitry, and a microcontroller interface to enable closed-loop control using external sensors.

The design emphasizes noise reduction, robust power delivery, and compact layout, making it suitable for robotics, automation, and aerospace applications. Special attention was given to PCB layout, grounding, and trace routing to ensure stable operation under load.
